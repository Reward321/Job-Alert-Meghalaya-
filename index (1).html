Welcome

Welcome to JAM: Job Alert Meghalaya.

This is a dedicated website built for job seekers and the youth of Meghalaya. Our goal is to provide reliable and authentic employment-related information.

We have a dedicated team that regularly updates the website to ensure that our youth receive genuine job opportunities.

Want updates? Join our official WhatsApp channel using the link above.

Our Goal

One of the most important goals of this website is to provide authentic employment news.

Many youths fall victim to job scams on platforms like Facebook and Instagram. We aim to stop scammers from exploiting people who are genuinely in need of jobs.

This website was built to protect job seekers and provide trusted employment opportunities.
